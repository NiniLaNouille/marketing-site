    For enterprise people

    Want to create a marketplace for your brand? Need custom features or support?› Let's talk

    * We provide the hosting for your marketplace and take care of all technical issues. When we release new features, your marketplace is updated automatically. You can just sit back and relax.
    ** Payment Gateway fees apply. We use PayPal as our payment gateway. Their fees depend on your country and sales volume and are usually around 2.5%-3.5%
    Prices do not include VAT. If you are an individual in EU or a company in Finland, 24% VAT will be added to the price. In other cases you don't need to pay VAT.

    Who is a member? Everybody can browse the content in your marketplace without creating an account. However, when somebody wants to either sell or buy something, they need to register as a member.

    What is a custom domain? By default the web address of your marketplace is in format "yourdomain.sharetribe.com". However, it's possible for you to use your own domain (eg. www.yourmarketplace.com) instead. This feature is available in all our plans except the "band" plan.

    What happens if I want to leave? If you're not 100% satisfied with our services, you're free to leave at any point. We will export all your data for you, and even provide our code with instructions to set it up on your own server. There's no lock-in of any kind in our services.
