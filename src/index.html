    Marketplace types

    Product selling, rentals, services - we have it all.

    Need a marketplace to sell products? Is your idea to facilitate peer-to-peer rentals? Or maybe a site for booking services? No worries - we support all types of marketplaces! And your merchants and buyers can be individuals or companies.

    Global payments made simple. Setup takes only a few minutes.

    Sharetribe's built-in payment system allows your merchants to accept both PayPal and all of major credit cards. It works in 190+ countries and in 25+ currencies. You can easily charge a fee from each transaction.
